Serotype Ranking & Bland‑Altman (piaB vs dominant assay)

Upload a CSV with first column = piaB (numeric Ct), remaining columns = serotype assays (Ct values).
For each sample, the assay with the lowest Ct < 40 is considered dominant (rank 1).
The Bland‑Altman plot compares piaB (method A) versus the Ct of the dominant assay (method B).
Points are colored by the dominant serotype name.
